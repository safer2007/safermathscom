How to Generate TOC

10 Aug 2021

The jekyll-gitbook theme leverages jekyll-toc to generate the Contents for the page. The TOC feature is not enabled by default. To use the TOC feature, modify the TOC configuration in _config.yml:

toc:
    enabled: true
